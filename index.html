Our History

Founded in 2024, CRIAR began with a dream to bring modern elegance to everyday wear. What started as a homegrown label quickly evolved into a beloved brand known for its quality and unique sense of style. Our journey began with just a few designs and a lot of passion — and today, we’re a movement of creativity and culture.

Our Mission

At CRIAR, we believe fashion is a voice. We strive to create pieces that blend timeless design with modern purpose. Whether it’s streetwear or ethnic fusion, every item we create supports sustainable craftsmanship and empowers self-expression.

Our Team

Our creative team is a diverse group of designers, developers, and dreamers. From fabric specialists to branding experts, each member brings a unique voice to CRIAR’s identity. Together, we create more than clothes — we craft stories people wear proudly.
